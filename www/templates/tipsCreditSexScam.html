Credit-for-Sex Scam

Tip #1: Be wary of strangers who befriend you online and those who offer escort, massage or sexual services on social media

Tip #2: Do not provide personal details about yourself when engaging with other internet users

Tip #3: Do not share your payment receipts containing details such as PINs with anyone

Tip #4: If you have any information relating to this scam, do not hestitate to call the Police hotline at 1800-255 0000, or dial ‘999’ for urgent Police assistance.
