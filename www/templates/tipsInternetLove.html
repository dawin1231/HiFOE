Internet Love Scam

Tip #1: Exercise caution when befriending strangers through the internet

Tip #2: Do not send money to people you do not know well. Scammers may spend months building an online relationship with you before asking for money

Tip #3:Contact the Police immediately if you receive any message or call from anyone claiming he/she is in some kind of trouble overseas and need you to urgently send money over

Tip #4: Inform the Police if anyone tries to extort money from you
