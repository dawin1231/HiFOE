Cyber Extortion

Tip #1: Exercise caution when befriending strangers on the internet

Tip #2: Do not agree to any request that may put you in a vulnerable position, such as performing compromising acts in front of a webcam and providing personal details to other internet users

Tip #3: Contact the Police immediately if you receive any message or call from anyone claiming he/she is in some kind of trouble overseas and need you to urgently send money over

Tip #4: Inform the Police if anyone tries to extort money from you
